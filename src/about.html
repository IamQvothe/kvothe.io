► About

Spending most of my youth graveily ill I spent a majority of my time indoors and this is where I quickly found my passion for learning and technology. I found myself with a computer in my hands at the ripe age of nine and a hunger for knowledge as I could not attend school due to being as sick as I was.
I took full advantage of the internet learning whatever my hearts desired, albeit a bit out of scope for a nine year old. I learned physics, chemistry, biology, I had interests in medicine, music, economics. I eventually found myself with coding languages.
As life happens I did not immediately pursue this interest until later in life but now have found myself in a position to put all my efforts in my ability to learn again and I have an opportunity to do what I have always wanted. Build. Something. Amazing.
